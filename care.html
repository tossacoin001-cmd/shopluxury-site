Shipping & delivery

  • Lagos: same day. Order before 2 PM WAT and she arrives tonight.
  • Nigeria: 1 to 3 days. Tracked, nationwide.
  • Worldwide: 5 to 9 days. Fully tracked, duties included to most destinations.
  • Friday drops ship from Monday. New pieces land on the site at 10 AM WAT every Friday.

You'll receive a tracking link by WhatsApp the moment she leaves the atelier.

Returns & exchanges

  • Exchanges within 7 days of delivery, unworn with tags attached.
  • Sale and made-to-order pieces are final. She's made just for you.
  • For hygiene, bodysuits and bottoms can't be returned once tried on.
  • To start an exchange, message your order number to the concierge and we'll arrange pickup or drop-off.

Something not right with your order? Tell us within 48 hours and we'll make it right, quickly.

Questions, answered

How do I place an order?
Add your pieces to the bag and checkout opens a WhatsApp chat with your full order ready to send. Confirm your details with your stylist and pay by transfer, card or on delivery in Lagos.
Which currencies can I pay in?
Prices show in USD or NGN. Switch any time using the toggle in the navigation. International orders are settled in USD; Nigerian orders in Naira.
Do you ship outside Nigeria?
Yes. From Lekki to London, Houston to Dubai. Worldwide delivery is fully tracked, 5–9 days, with duties included to most destinations.
Can I get styling help before I buy?
Always. Send your occasion to the concierge and your stylist replies with the look: sizing, pairing and all.
Are pieces restocked?
Most are limited runs. When she's gone, she's gone. Join the Inner Circle for first access and back-in-stock alerts.
